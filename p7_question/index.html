ANSWERS FOR QUESTIONS

How to Keep Safe During a House Fire

1 - Stop, drop, and roll if your clothes catch fire.
2 - If your clothes catch fire, immediately stop what you're doing, drop flat to the ground, and roll around until you put the fire out.
3 - Rolling around will smother the fire quickly.
4 - Cover your face with your hands as you're rolling to protect yourself.

How to Do Basic First Aid

1 - Protect yourself from bloodborne pathogens.
2 - Stop the bleeding first
3 - Treat shock next.
4 - Provide first aid for a broken bone.
5 - Help a choking victim.
6 - Learn how to treat a burn.
7 - Look out for a concussion.
8 - Treat a Spinal Injury Victim.

How to Do a Fireman's Carry

1 - Make sure that there's no possibility of spinal injury before moving the victim.
2 - Roll the victim onto their stomach if they're on their back.
3 - Hook your elbows under the victim's armpits
4 - Raise the victim to their feet.
5 - Place your right leg between the victim's legs.
6 - Grab the victim's right hand with your left.
7 - Squat down.
8 - Wrap your right arm around the back of the victim's right knee.
9 - Rise up and lift the victim's right thigh over your right shoulder.
10 - Move the victim.
